Visualization 3

Interpretation

In this visualization, we can see the top 5 incident categories that occur the most during each weekday. Each stacked bar has its incident category name, as well as the "rank" it holds on the top 5 for that weekday.

Discussion

This visualization was quite hard to do in Tableau, and there are some minor details I wished I had changed such as the order of the stacked bars. It also required more data manipulation than the other visualizations.
